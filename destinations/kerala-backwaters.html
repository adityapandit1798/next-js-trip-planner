Kerala Backwaters

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala's backwaters

Trip Overview

4 Days
Kerala, India
Max 10 people

Explore the tranquil backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat. This 4-day journey includes luxury accommodation on a private houseboat, authentic Kerala cuisine, and cultural experiences in local villages.

Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to Kerala

Arrive in Kochi, transfer to Alleppey, and board your luxury houseboat.

Day 2: Backwater Cruise

Full day cruising through scenic backwaters, visiting local villages and temples.

Day 3: Village Life & Culture

Experience local life, try traditional fishing techniques, and enjoy cultural performances.

Day 4: Farewell Kerala

Morning yoga, local market visit, and departure with lasting memories.
