Taj Mahal, Agra

Experience the majestic beauty of India's most iconic monument

Trip Overview

3 Days
Agra, India
Max 15 people

Discover the breathtaking Taj Mahal, one of the world's most beautiful monuments. This 3-day tour includes guided visits to the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and other historical sites, along with luxury accommodations and authentic local experiences.

Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Evening Tour

Arrive in Agra, check into your hotel, and enjoy an evening heritage walk through the old city markets.

Day 2: Taj Mahal Sunrise & Agra Fort

Watch the sunrise at the Taj Mahal, followed by a guided tour. After lunch, explore the magnificent Agra Fort.

Day 3: Local Culture & Departure

Visit local artisans, enjoy a cooking demonstration, and depart with memorable experiences.
